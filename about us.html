The Problem

Detecting Airport Security Threats is still exceedingly difficult,even in a post 9-11, increased security world. "Threat-hunting analysts require advanced anomaly detection and machine learnning capability to examine. 3d assets to scan luggage, vehicles, aircarft, and contraband."​

Goals

Create machine learning solutions that not only addresses the gaps in Airport security, but acts as a preventative measure against planned action; This in turn should aid TSA in identifying threats, linking suspicious individuals to possible contraband and allowing for discrete discovery of any possible threats.​
